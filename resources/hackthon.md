Bilkul, sources mein di gayi tamam tafseelat ko Roman Urdu mein neechay points ki soorat mein wazay kiya gaya hai taake aap ko poori picture samajh aa jaye:

### **1. Immediate Actions aur Timeline (Zaroori Tareekhein)**
*   **Selection Deadline (May 15):** Aap ko diay gaye 4 challenges mein se **sirf aik** select kar ke form bharna hai. Aik baar select karne ke baad aap track tabdeel nahi kar saktay.
*   **Submission Deadline (May 20):** Final project submit karne ki aakhri tareekh hai.
*   **Regional Finals (May 25–26):** Shortlisted teams ka virtual pitching round hoga.
*   **National Finale (June 07):** Islamabad mein final event hoga (Lahore/Karachi ki teams ko logistics support milegi).

### **2. Technical Core: Google Antigravity (Mandatory Requirements)**
Google Antigravity sirf aik tool nahi balkay aap ke system ka **main orchestrator** hona chahiye.
*   **Agentic Behavior:** Aap ka AI sirf summary na banaye balkay **reasoning, planning, aur action** kare.
*   **Action Simulation:** Har challenge mein kam az kam aik real-world action simulate karna laazmi hai (jaise mock API calls, database update, ya email/SMS bhejna).
*   **Logs/Traces:** Aap ko Antigravity se nikalne wale logs submit karne honge jo AI ka **Workplan, Reasoning steps, aur Decision flow** dikhayein.
*   **External Tools:** Aap Vertex AI, LangGraph, ya CrewAI use kar saktay hain, lekin unhein **Antigravity** ke zariye connect karna hoga.

### **3. Challenges ki Tafseel (The 4 Tracks)**

**Challenge 1: Autonomous Content-to-Action Agent**
*   **Maqsad:** Unstructured data (reports, news, spreadsheets) se insights nikal kar action lena.
*   **Requirements:** Kam az kam 5 sources se data lena, contradictions (ghalat fehmiyan) dhoondna, aur 3-5 interconnected actions generate karna.
*   **Example:** Sales report mein kami dekh kar khud hi discount campaign plan karna aur uska impact dikhana.

**Challenge 2: AI Service Orchestrator (Informal Economy)**
*   **Maqsad:** Plumbers, tutors, aur electricians jaise logon ke liye service booking automate karna.
*   **Requirements:** **Urdu aur Roman Urdu** requests ko samajhna, 6 se zyada factors (distance, rating, price) par providers ko rank karna, aur booking/follow-up sambhalna.
*   **Example:** User kahe "G-13 mein AC wala chahiye" aur system behtareen provider dhoond kar slot book kare.

**Challenge 3: Crisis Intelligence & Response Orchestrator (CIRO)**
*   **Maqsad:** Shehri masail (floods, accidents) ka fori response dena.
*   **Requirements:** Social media, weather, aur traffic signals ko fuse karna, crisis ki severity check karna, aur emergency alerts ya traffic rerouting simulate karna.

**Challenge 4: Agentic Game Quest**
*   **Maqsad:** Aisi mobile game jo player ke behavior ke mutabiq badalti rahay.
*   **Requirements:** **Adaptive Difficulty** (player ki skill ke hisaab se game mushkil ya asaan karna), AI NPCs jo player ki strategy se seekhein, aur procedural level generation.

### **4. Submission Checklist (Kya jama karana hai?)**
Aap ki final submission mein yeh cheezain hona laazmi hain:
1.  **Working Mobile App:** Mobile app prototype banana zaroori hai (Expo/React Native/Flutter allowed hain).
2.  **Demo Video:** 3 se 5 minute ki video jo end-to-end workflow dikhaye.
3.  **Antigravity Traces:** AI ki "soch" ka record (Workplan, Reasoning steps, Decision flow).
4.  **README/Documentation:** Architecture, setup steps, cost analysis, aur **Baseline Comparison** (yeh dikhana ke AI aam logic se behtar kyun hai).

### **5. Rules aur Evaluation Criteria**
*   **Eligibility:** Team mein 2 se 5 members hon aur umar 18 se 45 saal ho.
*   **Credits:** Har member ko $5 Google Cloud credits milenge, aur zaroorat parne par mazeed bhi mil saktay hain.
*   **Marks Breakdown:** Sab se zyada marks **Antigravity integration (20-30%)** aur **Agentic Reasoning (20-25%)** ke hain.
*   **Robustness:** Aap ko dikhana hoga ke aap ka system ghalat data ya API failure ko kaise handle karta hai.
